Ways to Earn Money

In DONKEY SMP, there are several exciting ways to earn in-game money, which can be used for purchasing items, ranks, and more. One of the primary methods is by killing mobs. Each mob you defeat rewards you with money, allowing you to gradually build your wealth.

Our server utilizes plugins such as Xconomy, Vault, and MobCash to manage the in-game economy effectively. These tools ensure a fair and balanced financial system, making your efforts to earn money enjoyable and rewarding.

Dive into battles against various creatures and enhance your skills while increasing your wealth in the game!
