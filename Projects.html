Ongoing Projects

At DONKEY SMP, we pride ourselves on our community-driven projects. Our server members are continuously engaged in exciting builds and challenges that bring everyone together.

Currently, we are working on a magnificent Castle that will serve as a central hub for all players. This grand structure will be a sight to behold and a place for events.

We are also creating a Museum that will showcase rare items and artifacts collected throughout our adventures, allowing players to reminisce about their journeys.

For the thrill-seekers, we have a project to Kill the Dragon, a challenging endeavor that requires teamwork and strategy.

We aim to face the powerful Wither Warden in a cooperative boss battle, testing our strength and skills as a community.

Lastly, a PVP Arena is under construction for those who enjoy competitive play. Gather your friends and engage in epic battles!
