Ranks Available

At DONKEY SMP, we offer a variety of ranks that players can purchase to unlock exclusive perks and stand out in the community.

The Player rank is the default for all users, giving you access to the basic features of our server. As you progress, you can obtain the Moderator rank, which allows you to assist in managing server activities and help your fellow players.

If you're looking for more control, the Admin rank provides full administrative access to the server tools and features.

For those who want to enjoy exclusive benefits, the VIP rank unlocks special areas and commands. The Elite VIP rank provides even more privileges, while the Legend rank offers the highest tier of access to unique features.

Additionally, the Seller rank allows you to participate in the in-game economy and sell items via auctions, providing further opportunities to earn and trade with other players.
